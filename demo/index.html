Limitations & Scientific Honesty judge-trust guardrails
Candidate ice, not confirmed ice

SAR-bright and polarimetric patterns are treated as evidence-consistent targets. They do not confirm water ice without calibrated CPR/DOP and external validation.

LOLA is coarse validation

NASA LRO/LOLA at 1000 m/pixel is used as a regional sanity check for terrain behavior, not meter-scale landing hazard certification.

Cold-trap proxy needs ephemeris validation

Low-sun hillshade simulations help screen plausible cold traps, but final PSR and illumination claims need ephemeris-grade polar illumination products.

OHRC overlap needs footprint registration

OHRC strips are high-resolution visual context until exact footprint registration confirms overlap with the selected DFSAR/TMC-2 AOI.
